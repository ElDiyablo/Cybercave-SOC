Advisory June 2026 — Cybercave SOC Team

⚠ Active Campaign: Financial Aid Phishing Targeting MSU Students

Action Required: If you received a suspicious financial aid email and clicked the link or entered credentials, change your university password immediately and report it to the SOC.

Summary

The Cybercave SOC has identified an active phishing campaign targeting MSU students. Attackers are sending emails impersonating the Financial Aid office, claiming that recipients must verify their FAFSA information or risk losing aid disbursements. The emails direct victims to a convincing but fraudulent login page designed to harvest university credentials.

Indicators of Compromise

# Sender domains observed (not legitimate MSU domains)
financialaid-msu-verify.com
msu-studentaid.net

# Subject lines observed
"[URGENT] FAFSA Verification Required — Aid Hold Applied"
"Action Required: Financial Aid Disbursement Verification"

What to Do

Do not click links in the email

Go directly to the official financial aid portal by typing the URL manually in your browser.

Report the email

Forward suspicious emails to the SOC via the incident form and to university IT.

If you clicked: change your password now

Go to your university account settings and change your password. Enable MFA if you haven't already.

Check for unauthorized access

Review your account's recent login history for any unfamiliar locations or devices.
