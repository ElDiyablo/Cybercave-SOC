⚠ Active Campaign: Financial Aid Phishing Targeting MSU Students

The SOC has observed an increase in phishing emails impersonating the Financial Aid office. Emails claim FAFSA verification is required and direct victims to a credential-harvesting page. All students should verify any financial aid communications directly through the official portal.

📅 May 2026 Threat Report By SOC Team

Password Spray Activity Detected on Campus VPN

SOC analysts identified a low-and-slow password spray attack against campus VPN authentication endpoints. The attack originated from multiple external IPs and targeted common usernames. No successful logins were confirmed. Detection rule deployed and IPs blocked at the perimeter.
